Sharbot Fermentation Lifecycle

Posted on July 1, 2025

Project Overview:

Fermentation is a complex biochemical process. "Sharbot" is a traditional drink that undergoes a unique two-stage fermentation: first converting sugar to ethanol (anaerobic), and then ethanol to acetic acid/vinegar (aerobic).

Key Objectives

  • Model yeast dynamics and biomass growth.
  • Simulate the inhibition effects of pH and alcohol concentration.
  • Design logic to switch between anaerobic and aerobic stages automatically.

Implementation
